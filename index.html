    About us

    Hello! We are Mustang Makeover. We are Suri Choi, Emi Matsumoto, and Lily Stone-Bourgeois. Our goal is to contribute to making ASIJ a more sustainable campus. By cleaning up trash and giving old items away, we hope to make students more aware of over-consumption and waste. Join us in our fight for the future!

    • Looking for a way to help the ASIJ community and commit acts of service? This is where Mustang Makeover comes in. Sign up for shifts to clean up around the school. Sign up the day of or before, and you must submit a before and after picture of an area you picked up trash from. Post it on the feed to get credit!
    • Looking for somewhere to donate your old items? We have a solution: Mustang Memories. Post your old items on the giveaway page! Add your email underneath, and change the status depending on the situation.

    Functionality

    A user uploads two photos (one before and one after picking up trash), and the post is displayed on the website feed with the user’s ASIJ school email as the “username”. The website has four pages: the home page, the sign-up page, the Mustang Makeover page, and the Mustang Memories page.

    Objective

    As students, we have noticed the trash that gets left around campus. Our objective is to clean up the school for a more eco-friendly environment while bringing the students aware of the amount of waste they use. In this way, ASIJ can help students make more eco-conscious choices. Our other objective revolves around the overconsumption that takes place at ASIJ. In an age with highly rapid trends, students buy a lot that they stop using after a couple of months. By establishing a place for students to post items open to other students, we hope to promote a sustainable reusing system.
